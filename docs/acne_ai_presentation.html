ACNE AI 07 / 15

Training Strategy

2-Phase Transfer Learning + Progressive Resizing

Phase 1: Feature Extraction (300×300)

  • V2-S backbone completely frozen
  • 25 epochs at 300×300 (fast convergence)
  • Mixup + CutMix augmentation
  • Cosine warmup: 1e-6 → 1e-3

Phase 2: Fine-Tuning (380×380)

  • Top 70% of layers unfrozen
  • Progressive resize to 380×380
  • Up to 80 epochs (early stopping, patience=20)
  • Cosine annealing: 1e-7 → 3e-5
Class Weighting Label Smoothing 0.15 Mixup + CutMix AdamW Optimizer Google Colab T4 Mixed Precision FP16
